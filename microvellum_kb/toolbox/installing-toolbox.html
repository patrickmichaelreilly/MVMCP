Installing Toolbox

Below is a systematic guide for installing Microvellum Toolbox. Before installation, we recommend reviewing our  Pre-Installation Instructions  and  System Requirements.


1. Right-click the Setup.exe file and select “Run as administrator."

2. An interface containing the End-User License Agreement (EULA) will appear, read and check box to agree to the Terms of Use, click next. 



3. Configure the desired installation path and select "Install". 



4. Once the installation reaches 100% a notification will appear to restart your computer to finish the installation process. You do not have to restart your computer immediately but will need to restart your computer to finish the installation process.



5. A Toolbox shortcut icon will now appear on your desktop.

This article explained how to install Toolbox software on your desktop.
