Assigning a Toolfile to a Processing Station

This article explains the steps to assign a toolfile to a processing station.

  1. Hover over "Toolbox Setup" and select "Options." 


  2. Select the "Processing Stations" tab and double click the processing station you would like to assign a toolfile to. 


  3. In the "Settings" tab, observe the toolfile section. Select "Edit" to edit the current toolfile or "Change" to assign a toolfile. 


  4. After assigning a toolfile, observe the toolfile column is updated with the assigned or edited toolfile.
