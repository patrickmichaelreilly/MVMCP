Parts List Review

The following article is an overview of the Parts List Review feature. 

The Parts List Review feature allows you to review the components of a product before creating a Work Order. This interface provides a preview of the parts included in a product, enabling you to verify their accuracy and ensure that everything is ready for production when you are ready to create a Work Order.

How to Access 

There are two options to access the Parts List Review:
1. Select the Product List in the Project tab of the Microvellum palette. 
2. Right click on a product in the list and select Parts List Review from the quick menu.



1. Select the Modify tab in the Microvellum palette and then select the Modify Products section.
2. Click on the Parts List Review button and select the desired products in your drawing to view and press Enter.



The interface allows you to view the materials, specification groups, dimensions, and edge banding that have been assigned to each product. By reviewing the parts list before processing work orders, you can ensure that your products have the correct materials and dimensions, which helps to maintain accuracy in the shop.
