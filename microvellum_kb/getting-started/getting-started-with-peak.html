Getting Started with peAk

This article provides a step-by-step guide to accessing peAk, INNERGY's all-in-one learning platform.
Follow this link for an instructional video that reiterates the information found in this article. 

Step 1: Access the peAk Platform

  1. Open your browser and go to https://peak.innergy.com/pages/97/welcome-to-bricscad-training.
  2. Alternatively, use the direct link provided in your welcome email.

Step 2: Log In

  1. Use your email address and the temporary password included in the welcome email.
  2. Upon your first login, you’ll be prompted to create a new password for your account. Once you have changed your password, you will be prompted to log in again with your newly created password.

Step 3: Explore Your Dashboard

  1. After updating your password, you’ll land on the peAk dashboard.
  2. This is your central hub for all available learning content, including courses designed to help you get up to speed with BricsCAD.

Step 4: Access BricsCAD Courses

  1. Look for the “English BricsCAD courses” section.
  2. Click the “Learn Now!” button to proceed.
French-language version of this content will be available soon.

Step 5: Choose a Learning Plan

  1. Browse through the list of available learning plans.
  2. Click on a learning plan that interests you.

  1. Once you have opened the learning plan, click “Enroll” on the right-hand side.


Step 6: Begin Your Learning

You have two options:
  1. To start the full learning plan: Click “Start Learning Now”. 

  1. To take an individual course: Click the “Play” button next to the specific course you want to access.

Step 7: Inside a Course

  1. Once inside, click “Start Learning Now” to begin the course content.


If you run into any login issues, please reach out to peak@innergy.com for assistance.
