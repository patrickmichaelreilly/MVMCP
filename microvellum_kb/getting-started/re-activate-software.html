Asking to Re-Activate Software on Start Up

If your license is current, but you keep getting the Activate Software window when starting up Toolbox, this is the fix. Instead of entering your serial number and password every time, go to your MV Toolbox icon on your desktop and Right-click then Run as administrator. Once the program starts, you can close and open it again the way you would normally.
This will stop the window from appearing until your license expires.
