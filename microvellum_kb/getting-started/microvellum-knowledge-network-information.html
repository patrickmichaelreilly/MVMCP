Microvellum Knowledge Network Information

24-Hour Support Access

Microvellum provides users with active support & maintenance subscriptions with access to our support channels 24-hours a day via email, online community, and phone. From our interactive support site, you can find answers to common issues, communicate with other users from around the world and Create Support Cases for our software support agents.  Access live support representatives any time, day or night.
  1. North America: 1.800.204.0913
  2. Australia: 1800.821.797
  3. New Zealand: 0800-578-290
  4. United Kingdom: 0800 680 0669

Free Product Updates

Your Support & Maintenance Subscription entitles you to free access to product updates and beta releases. Stay current and make use of the latest advancements developed to help improve your productivity and efficiency.  Check out the latest software Release Notes, then request an update.

Free Product Library Updates

Our Product Engineers are working hard to ensure our product libraries are maintained to take full advantage of the advancements in our software. We provide users with active support & maintenance subscriptions full access to Product Library Expansions. On a monthly basis, we meet with users of our software to discuss the latest changes and additions to Microvellum's Foundation Library.  

Knowledge Base

Gain access to our growing product Knowledge Base and convenient tips & tricks, product documentation, how-to videos and other helpful information.

Free Video Training Courses

Gain access to video-based training courses designed to provide you with basic, intermediate and advanced insight into Microvellum’s software and product library data. 

Community Forums

Find answers. Share ideas. Solve problems. The Microvellum Knowledge Network helps you get the most out of your software investment – putting you in touch with software experts and other users from around the world.

Detailed Support History

Keep track of your entire Case History in one convenient location.  Find answers to previously reported issues and keep tabs on existing open cases.
