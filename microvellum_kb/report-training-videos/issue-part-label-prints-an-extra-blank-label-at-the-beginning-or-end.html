Issue: Part Label Prints an Extra Blank Label at the Beginning or End

Symptom

You have created a custom part label, but when you print the labels, an extra blank label is printed at the beginning or end of the series of labels. 

Resolution 1

If the label contains group header bands, set the GroupHeaderBand property "KeepGroupHeader" to False.

Resolution 2

Adjust the databand height property downward by small increments until the blank page is eliminated. Be careful not to change the height too much, or the label may print off-center.

Resolution 3

If the label contains a group header band, set the GroupHeaderBand properties "New Page Before" and "New Page After" both to False.
