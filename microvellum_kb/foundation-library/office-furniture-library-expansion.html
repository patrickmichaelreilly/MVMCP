Office Furniture Library Expansion

Office Furniture Library Expansion

The Microvellum Office Furniture Library contains an in-depth collection of furniture products. This library is intended as a foundation for manufacturers to establish their own catalog. Products can be created and modified to suit an individual’s unique needs.

As is, the library boasts over a hundred global variables, and each product contains more than a dozen unique variables. It has a database that supports either Imperial OR Metric units. The Office Furniture Library is the first Microvellum library developed from the ground up to be used internationally, allowing data developers to share intellectual product development across the globe!

For installation and purchasing, please contact your account manager.
