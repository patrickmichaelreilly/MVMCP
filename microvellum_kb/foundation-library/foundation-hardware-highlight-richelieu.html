Foundation Hardware Highlight: Richelieu

 
Microvellum’s Foundation Library is built on the premise of providing our users with the best quality hardware for their construction projects, be they commercial, residential, or anything otherwise. As such, Toolbox has hardware from multiple brands integrated into its extensive library catalogue, to best reflect the most popular hardware currently available on the market. 

Richelieu is a leading manufacturer and distributor of specialty hardware based in North America, with a large catalog of products used across multiple industries. Microvellum has incorporated a sizable amount of Richelieu hardware into the Foundation Library, including drawer slides, drawer systems, hinges, and a lift system, comprising over 4% of the Foundation Library’s overall catalog of hardware at the time of writing. 

Drawer Slides

Microvellum’s Foundation Library, at the time of writing, contains 2 major lines of concealed Richelieu brand drawer slides, with two major lines: the Richelieu Evolution series (comprised of the Evolution 815 and 818), and standard concealed Richelieu slides (comprised of the 823, 825, and 828). 
Evolution Series

The Richelieu Evolution series are a set of undermount drawer slides with a built-in soft close mechanism, telescopic movement, and a load bearing capacity of 35 kg (77 lbs.) All slides come in pairs and can be added to any corresponding drawer box or drawer system. For more information on the Richelieu Evolution series, please visit Richelieu’s official website.

Drawer Systems

Microvellum's Foundation Library contains 3 Richelieu brand drawer systems. The options listed here are available to users by default, open for use by enabling "Drawer Options" under the product prompts, or else can be set via the global variables. At the time of writing, the Richelieu brand drawer systems available are: the Richelieu Axial, 908, and Optimiz-R. 
Optimiz-R

The Richelieu Optimiz-R is a metal drawer system with slim double paneled walls designed to fit into a wide range of furniture applications. Designed to maximize storage space, the Optimiz-R is designed to be installed in conjunction with Richelieu brand slides, such as the 828, with a load bearing capacity of 45kg (100 lbs.) For more information on the Richelieu Optimiz-R, please visit Richelieu’s official website.

Hinges

Microvellum’s Foundation Library, at the time of writing, carries a variety of hinges belonging to a singular Richelieu product line: the Richelieu RCS hinges. This series of hinges can be applied to both frameless and face frame products.
RCS Hinges

The Richelieu RCS series of hinges are a set of adjustable Euro hinges with a wide range of angles and configurations to ensure that there is a hinge for any application, be it full overlay, half overlay, inset, or more. The hinges come with an optional soft-closing mechanism, and configurations for both frameless and face frame cabinetry. To learn more about Richelieu’s RCS series of hinges, please visit Richelieu’s official website. 
The Microvellum Foundation Library carries the RCS hinges with the following options for client usage and customization: 

7 different hinge options:
  1. RCS 30°
  2. RCS 45°
  3. RCS 95°
  4. RCS 95 Mini°
  5. RCS 103°
  6. RCS 105°
  7. RCS 165°
Face Frame Overlay Hinge Options available in with Dowel or Screw-On.
Full range of mounting plates with options for:
  1. Eccentric Adjustment
  2. Face Frame
  3. Euro Screws
  4. Screw-In
  5. Expando
All options can be modified to either inset, full overlay, or half overlay.
All options have configurations for soft-closing or spring-closing.
