Toolbox Recommended Workstations

This article provides a list of laptop and desktop workstations with the performance and power needed to run Microvellum Toolbox and similar applications.  All of these machines meet or exceed our System Requirements for Toolbox.  See What to Look for in a New Workstation for Toolbox and AutoCAD for more details. 
Workstations are sectioned by brand and listed in order of performance for each type, with the highest performance machines listed last. This list reflects recommended hardware for OEM 2024 and older, as of July of 2024.
