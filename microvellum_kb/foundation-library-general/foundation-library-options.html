Inset - Overlay Options

Most products contained within this library will have options to control the placement of various parts (as seen below).

  1. Top Rear Overhang – Enter the amount the top overhangs the rear of the cabinet. 
  2. Top Front Overhang – Enter the amount the top overhangs the front of the cabinet. 
  3. Top Left Overhang – Enter the amount the top overhangs the left of the cabinet. 
  4. Top Right Overhang – Enter the amount the top overhangs the right of the cabinet. 
  5. Inset Back – Enter the value to Inset the Back of Cabinet. 
  6. Inset Top and Bottom – Enter the amount you require the top and bottom to be inset. 
  7. Inset Shelves – Enter the amount you require the shelves to be inset from the front of the cabinet. 
  8. Inset Toe Kick – Enter Value to Inset the Toe Kick of Cabinet. 
  9. Modesty Panel Inset – Enter the distance the modesty panels sit back from the back edge of the legs.

Material Options

Materials for each product in this library can be modified using the Product Prompts and checking the Show Material Options in the lower right corner. In the Material Options for the specific product, you are able to change the material for each individal part and change Grain Direction.
