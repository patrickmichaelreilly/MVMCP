Foundation Hardware Highlight: Hettich


 
Microvellum’s Foundation Library is built on the premise of providing our users with the best quality hardware for their construction projects, be they commercial, residential, or anything otherwise. As such, Toolbox has hardware from multiple brands integrated into its extensive library catalogue, to best reflect the most popular hardware currently available on the market. 

Hettich is an internationally popular company, with hardware that is utilized by manufacturers and designers worldwide, well known for creating adaptable, heavily customizable products that are easily assembled and often have interchangeable components to allow for adaptation to suit construction needs. Microvellum has incorporated a significant amount of Hettich hardware into the Foundation Library, including drawer slides, drawer systems, folding and sliding door systems, hinges, lift systems, pullouts, wall hangers, hanging rails, and garment lifts, all of which collectively comprises roughly 20% of the Foundation Library’s hardware catalogue (at the time of writing). The range of these components and lines varies depending on the specific products in question, but there is generally a significant enough number of options for users to be able to comfortably integrate Hettich brand hardware into any project.  

Drawer Slides

Microvellum’s Foundation Library, at the time of writing, contains 4 Hettich brand lines of drawer runner and slide systems, along with different variations of those systems: the KA ball bearing runner slide systems, the FR roller runner system, the Hettich Actro line (including the Actro 5D and the Actro YOU), and the Hettich Quadro line (including the Quadro V6, V6+, 4D V6, Quadro 25, and Quadro YOU). 
KA Slide System

The Hettich KA series are a line of ball bearing runners built with steel ball bearings enclosed in plastic for noise cancelling opening. The runners come in partial, full, and over extension, with load bearing capacities of 12-20 kg (partial), 90 kg (over extension), and 136 kg (full extension), with stop control, silent opening/closing, and push-to-open. For more information on the KA runner system, please visit Hettich’s official website.

Hettich Quadro

The Hettich Quadro is a customizable set of drawer slides that come in a wide variety of configurations. The slides come in partial or full extension, with options available for push-to-open, silent system, and stop control. The Foundation Library carries 4 different varieties of the Quadro: the Quadro 25, the Quadro V6, V6+, and 4D V6, all with the full range of options available. The V6, V6+, and 4D V6 are full extension slides intended for use with wooden drawers, with load bearing capacities of 45kg (V6/4D V6) and 60kg (V6+). For more information on the Quadro line of drawer slides, please visit Hettich’s official website.

InnoTech

The Hettich Innotech is a double walled drawer system with customizable side configuration. The InnoTech comes in two different forms: the basic Innotech, a classic double walled drawer system with rounded contours and a system for replaceable side configuration, and the InnoTech Atira, a contemporary drawer system with customizable designer profiles, internal panels, utilizing the DesignSides system for greater degrees of variety. For more information on InnoTech or InnoTech Atira, please visit Hettich’s official website.
