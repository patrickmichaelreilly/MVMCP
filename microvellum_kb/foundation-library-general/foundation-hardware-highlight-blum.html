Foundation Hardware Highlight: Blum

This article will list out all Blum hardware available for use in the Microvellum Foundation Library. 

Microvellum’s Foundation Library is built on the premise of providing our users with the best quality hardware for their construction projects, be they commercial, residential, or anything otherwise. As such, Toolbox has hardware from multiple brands integrated into its extensive library catalogue, to best reflect the most popular hardware currently available on the market. 

Blum is an internationally popular company in the furniture hardware trade, renowned for its versatile, flexible product kit system and expansive product lines. Microvellum has incorporated 3D recreations of a robust mixture of Blum brand drawer systems, drawer slides, hinges, lift systems, and door buffers/push catch systems, which collectively comprise roughly 23% of the current Foundation Library’s hardware catalogue.

The range of these components and lines varies depending on the specific products in question, but there is generally a significant enough number of options for users to be able to comfortably integrate Blum brand hardware into any project.  
The Library also contains Blum brand options for drawer sliders, hinges, the AVENTOS lift system, as well as door buffers/push catch systems. 

Blum Drawer Systems

Microvellum's Foundation Library contains several Blum brand drawer systems available for use. The options listed here are available to users by default, open for use by enabling "Drawer Options" under the product prompts of cabinetry or other projects containing drawers. Currently, the Library utilizes 3D recreations of the LEGRABOX, MERIVOBOX, METABOX, and TANDEMBOX systems, as well as the two plus product lines for TANDEMBOX, ANTARO AND INTIVO. The Foundation Library also offers clients use of the toe-kick based drawer/plinth, the SPACE STEP.

TANDEMBOX ANTARO

The lightweight, simplified and comparatively minimalistic iteration of the TANDEMBOX. To learn more about the ANTARO, please visit Blum's official website.

The Foundation Library contains the TANDEMBOX ANTARO drawer system with the following options for customization:
  1. 7 height options: 
    1. Height B
    2. Height C
    3. Height D
    4. Height K
    5. Height M
    6. Height N 
  2. D-Double Gallery (available for users who wish to create a drawer with closed sides). 
  3. 9 supported runner slider lengths:
    1. 270mm
    2. 300mm
    3. 350mm
    4. 400mm
    5. 450mm
    6. 500mm
    7. 550mm
    8. 600mm
    9. 650mm
  4. Support for inner drawer options 
  5. TIP-ON mechanism, allowing for handle-less drawers and cabinetry.  
  6. Load bearing capacities between 30/65 kg (66/143 lbs.) 
  7. Silk White or Terra Black Finishes.
