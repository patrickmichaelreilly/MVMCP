Tutorial: Working with Door Profile Trims

This video demonstrates an alternative to adding prompts and formulas when working with the size and 3D origin of door parts. The goal of this alternative is to minimize the complexity and clutter of the spreadsheets for data that some users will never need. It utilizes the existing ability of Microvellum to subtract material relative to the part edges that receive edgebanding.

This video demonstrates how to oversize door parts to create a part cut size that is different from the part finish size.
  1. How to set up the ‘new’ milling trim edgeband materials.  (begin – 01:17)
  2. How the milling trims are applied to part edges. (begin – 04:35)
