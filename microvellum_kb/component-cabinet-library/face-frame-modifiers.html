Face Frame Modifiers

The video featured in this article covers Face Frame Modifier Subassemblies, which include: 
  1. Bottom Rail Arch
  2. Bottom Rail Double Arch
  3. Bottom Rail Scallop
  4. Bottom Rail Three Sided Notch
  5. Left Stile Chamfer Corner Flutes 
  6. Left Stile Flutes and Profiles
  7. Right Stile Chamfer Corner Flutes
  8. Right Stile Flutes and Profiles
  9. Top Rail Arch
  10. Top Rail Double Arch
  11. Top Rail Scallop
  12. Top Rail Three Sided Notch

To access the subassembly face frame modifiers featured in this video, select Product Prompts > select the desired product.


Fig. 01 - Product Prompts

Right-click within the Product Prompts Interface and select Subassemblies. 


Fig. 02 - Subassemblies

From the Subassemblies Interface, the Face Frame Modifiers menu is located within the left-hand pane. 


Fig. 03 - Face Frame Modifiers Menu
