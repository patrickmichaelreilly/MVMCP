Foundation Library Catalog: Countertops

This document lists all products in the Countertop category of Microvellum's Foundation Library. Countertop products are products designed to be placed atop counters or placed along wall components to act as surfaces, coming in a variety of shapes to suit a wide range of different types of counters. Products in this category are primarily differentiated by their basic shape, with 2 of the 6 products also having built in appliance customization options. 

This document will reference “Standard Prompts”, referring to the set of prompts that are generally present in all products in the Microvellum Foundation Library catalog by default. These prompts will not be listed among the prompts of products, as they are considered default. Standard Prompts are the following:
  1. Width
  2. Height
  3. Depth
  4. Toe Kick Height
  5. Left/Right Filler Width
  6. Product Name
  7. Product Version
  8. Left/Right Finished End
  9. Left/Right Applied End
  10. Show Construction Options
  11. Show Material Options
  12. Show Advanced Options
  13. Show Flatshot Options
  14. Show 2D Drawing Options
  15. Show Item Number Options
  16. Show Rendering Options

Countertop Left L Shape


Product Category: Countertops

Product Description
The Countertop Left L Shape product is a countertop product designed to be placed at the top of other solids or against wall components, offering customization on the return of the countertop, as well as detailing of every available edge. The product has 5 edges that can be customized with different types of details. The countertop uses standard prompts, as well as prompts specific to countertop products.

Countertop With Substrate


Product Category: Countertops

Product Description
A countertop product with a built-in substrate and appliance options, allowing clients to select between a sink, stove, and cooktop. The product can optionally add waterfall ends and is designed to be placed against wall components and on top of other solids. This product uses standard prompts, as well as prompts specific to countertop products, and to customize the location of its built-in appliance.
