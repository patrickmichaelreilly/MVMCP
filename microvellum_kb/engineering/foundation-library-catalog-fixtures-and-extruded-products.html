Foundation Library Catalog: Fixtures and Extruded Products

This document lists the full range of extruded products and fixtures available in Microvellum's Foundation Library. Extruded products are products that drawn or extruded along a 2D polyline path, allowing for the creation of products in a wide variety of shapes, with many extruded products automatically creating separate segments based on the angle/shape of the line itself. Products within this category of the Foundation Library vary, with options for extruding countertops, die walls, cabinets, a toe kick base, moldings, as well as parts that can be combined and customized to create or add onto a new product. 


This document will reference “Standard Prompts”, referring to the set of prompts that are generally present in all products in the Microvellum Foundation Library catalog by default. These prompts will not be listed among the prompts of products, as they are considered default. Standard Prompts are the following:
  1. Width
  2. Height
  3. Depth
  4. Toe Kick Height
  5. Left/Right Filler Width
  6. Product Name
  7. Product Version
  8. Left/Right Finished End
  9. Left/Right Applied End
  10. Show Construction Options
  11. Show Material Options
  12. Show Advanced Options
  13. Show Flatshot Options
  14. Show 2D Drawing Options
  15. Show Item Number Options
  16. Show Rendering Options

Extruded Toe Kick Base


Product Category: Extruded Fixtures and Products- Extruded Fixtures

Product Description
The Extruded Toe Kick Base product is a toe kick base that is designed to conform to the shape of a given cabinet. The product has options for skin application (including adding multiple layers of skins) on the face and back of the product, as well as options for customizing the number of sleepers in the product (this is calculated by default based on the line the product was extruded along). As with most products, the Extruded Toe Kick Base utilizes standard prompt options, as well as options exclusive to extruded products such as setting of maximum wall length, segmenting of the product based on angle, and how much to offset (if at all) product position relative to the polyline path it was extruded along.
