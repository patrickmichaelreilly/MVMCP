Foundation Hardware Highlight: Grass


Microvellum’s Foundation Library is built on the premise of providing our users with the best quality hardware for their construction projects, be they commercial, residential, or anything otherwise. As such, Toolbox has hardware from multiple brands integrated into its extensive library catalogue, to best reflect the most popular hardware currently available on the market. 

Grass is an international and well-regarded company known for creating innovative and wide-ranging lines of hardware products used in furniture and kitchen manufacturing. Microvellum has incorporated a significant amount of Grass hardware into the Foundation Library, including drawer systems, drawer systems, drawer slides, hinges, as well as lift systems, all of which collectively comprise roughly 13% of the Foundation Library’s hardware catalogue (at the time of writing).

For more information on the products listed in this article, or to learn more about Grass as a company, visit Grass's official website (EU) (USA).

Grass’s slim-sided, rectangular drawer system designed with 90° straight corners and sides. The Vionaro’s sides are constructed from a single piece of steel laser welded together, designed to exclusively work with the Grass Dynapro series of drawer slides, with the slides built into the drawer itself.
